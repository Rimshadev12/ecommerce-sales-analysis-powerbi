## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of an e-commerce dataset using Power BI. The goal is to uncover key business insights related to sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Identify high-performing products and categories
* Evaluate customer retention and churn patterns
* Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* **Total Revenue:** $1.17 Billion
* **Total Orders:** 169K+
* **Total Customers:** 67K+
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** 6.91K

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

* Executive Sales Overview
* Monthly Revenue Trends
* Customer Acquisition Analysis
* Repeat vs One-time Customers
* Product Performance (Top Products & Pareto Analysis)
* Discount vs Revenue Analysis
* Customer Segmentation (RFM & LTV)
* Cohort Analysis for Customer Retention

---

## 🧠 Key Insights

* 📌 Revenue shows strong seasonality, peaking in November
* 📌 Top products contribute ~70% of total revenue (Pareto Principle)
* 📌 Majority of customers are one-time buyers → high churn risk
* 📌 Customer retention drops significantly after first purchase
* 📌 High-value customers generate most of the revenue

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Customer Retention Strategy

* Implement loyalty programs
* Use email/SMS marketing for re-engagement
* Offer personalized discounts

### 2. Revenue Growth Strategy

* Reduce dependency on seasonal sales
* Introduce campaigns during low-performing months

### 3. Product Optimization

* Focus on high-performing products
* Optimize inventory management
* Improve or remove low-performing products

### 4. Customer Targeting

* Target high-value customers with premium offers
* Convert medium-value customers into high-value
